Self Priming Centrifugal Feed Pump For RO

DISCRIPTION

The Lubi LCF pumps are Self Priming Centrifugal Feed Pump for Reverse Osmosis (RO). Motor and pump are close coupled in a convenient and compact design for quick installation in limited space. The pumps have axial suction port and radial discharge port .

APPLICATION

These pumps are suitable for liquids which are thin, clean, slightly aggressive, non-explosive, not containing solid particles or fibers.

• Reverse Osmosis (RO) systems

• For clean liquids without abrasives, which are non-aggressive for the pump materials. (solids content up to 0.2%)

•For water supply.

For heating, cooling, air-conditioning and circulation plants.

• For civil and industrial applications.

OPERATING CONDITIONS

Flow range : Up to 3.5 m³/h

Head range : Up to 35 metres

Ambient temperature : Max. +50°C

Liquid temperature range : 0°C to +90°C

MOTOR

•2-pole induction motor, 50 Hz (n = 2900 RPM)

•Single phase 230V +5% -15% with thermal protection

• Protection IP 44..

• Constructed in accordance with IEC 34.
