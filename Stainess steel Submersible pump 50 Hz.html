Stainess steel Submersible pump 50 Hz

DISCRIPTION

Stainless steel submersible Pump 100% made out of high grade stainless steel they can withstand the harsh abrasive and corrosive environment during its life span. The octagonal rubber bearings and built in sand protection shields are designed to ensure that sand is removed from the pump and motor by the water that is being pumped. Submersible pumps made out of materials other than stainless steel can run very well in cold, clean & clear water. However ground water contains abrasives like sand which can quickly wear out both the pump & motor. Lubi has hence designed & developed the J & W series Stainless steel Submersible Pump which made completely out of high grade stainless steel & rubber of premium quality to ensure that the suspended particles do not wear out the pump.
